Grooming & Body Language
Some of the perceptions people can form solely from your appearance: 
⚫ Your professionalism.
⚫ Your level of sophistication.
⚫ Your intelligence.
⚫ Your credibility. 
Business Attire
Three basic things you need to consider:
⚫ Your line of work
⚫ Your corporate culture
⚫ Your audience 
Before buying something to wear to work, ask yourself
⚫ Is it appropriate for the kind of job I have?
⚫ Is it a fad or will it be a basic wardrobe foundation?
⚫ Does it fit properly?
⚫ Will I stand out (in a positive light) if I wear it to work?
⚫ Do I feel successful and confident wearing it?
⚫ Would my boss wear it? 
Body Language
⚫ Eye contact
⚫ Facial expressions
⚫ Postures & gestures
⚫ Personal space
⚫ Bias and prejudice 
Few Tips
⚫ Use eye contact.
⚫ Avoid too much movement
⚫ Smile– Use expressions and hand movements
⚫ Hold your hands open and wide apart to show sincerity and honesty
⚫ Ensure your body language communicates "I am glad to be here"
